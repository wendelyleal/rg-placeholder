# Rollin’ Green — Brand System Playbook

## 1. Brand Core

**Positioning**  
A lifestyle-first cannabis brand that blends neighborhood accessibility with curated, premium experiences.

**Archetype**  
The Connector + The Insider

**Core Idea**  
"Cannabis as culture, not just product."

---

## 2. Visual System

### Color Tokens

**Primary**
- Deep Green (#0F3D2E approx)

**Accent**
- Signal Red (#E53935 approx)

**Secondary / Atmosphere**
- Soft Mint / Gradient Greens
- Light Sky Blue backgrounds

**Neutrals**
- White (#FFFFFF)
- Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
- Charcoal (#1A1A1A)

**Usage Rules**
- Green = base + trust
- Red = action only (CTA, highlights)
- Gradients = section separators and emotional transitions

---

### Typography System

**Primary Font**
- Modern sans-serif (Inter / Helvetica Now / similar)

**Hierarchy**
- H1: Bold, tight tracking
- H2: Semi-bold
- Body: Regular

**Stylistic Layer**
- Occasional display type (neon / glow effect)

**Rules**
- Avoid serif
- Avoid overly decorative fonts
- Maintain strong readability

---

## 3. Layout & UI Patterns

### Core Principles
- Rounded everything (8px–24px radius)
- Card-based layout
- Soft shadows (low opacity, wide blur)
- Section stacking with clear separation

### Components

**Buttons**
- Primary: Red background, white text
- Secondary: Green or outline
- Rounded (pill or soft rectangle)

**Cards**
- Elevated containers
- Product or content-focused

**Forms**
- Full-width inputs
- Soft borders
- Minimal friction

---

## 4. Imagery Direction

### Content Types
- Product photography (clean, isolated or floating)
- Lifestyle (subtle, not overly staged)
- Graphic environments (illustrations, gradients)

### Visual Motifs
- Neon signage (nightlife)
- Floating elements
- Organic shapes (leaves, curves)

### Rules
- Avoid overly dark or gritty imagery
- Maintain brightness and clarity
- Always balance realism + stylization

---

## 5. Motion & Interaction

### Motion Style
- Smooth, soft easing
- Subtle hover effects
- Scroll-based reveals

### Microinteractions
- Button hover = slight scale + shadow
- Cards = lift on hover

### Avoid
- Harsh animations
- Fast, aggressive transitions

---

## 6. Tone of Voice

### Voice Attributes
- Conversational
- Confident
- Slightly playful
- Community-driven

### Writing Style
- Short sentences
- Clear CTAs
- No jargon

### Messaging Patterns
- "Be the first"
- "Something is coming"
- "Stay tuned"

---

## 7. Content Blocks (Reusable)

### Hero Section
- Headline (bold, simple)
- Subheadline (supportive)
- CTA (red)
- Visual anchor (product or graphic)

### Hype Banner
- Dark or gradient background
- Strong statement
- Minimal text

### Product Grid
- Card layout
- Image + name + CTA

### Email Capture
- Friendly headline
- Minimal fields
- Strong CTA

---

## 8. Brand Experience Rules

### Must Always
- Feel welcoming
- Feel curated
- Encourage discovery

### Must Avoid
- Clinical tone
- Overly luxury tone
- Visual clutter

---

## 9. Implementation Blueprint

### When Designing a Page
1. Start with a soft gradient or neutral base
2. Add a strong hero with one focal message
3. Stack sections (cards, banners, grids)
4. Insert CTAs at natural breaks
5. Use red sparingly

### When Designing a Feature
- Wrap everything in cards
- Maintain spacing consistency
- Use motion to guide attention

---

## 10. One-Line Creative Direction

**"Build a cannabis brand that feels like a premium neighborhood spot with nightlife energy and a clean digital-first experience."**
